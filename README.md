# HR Analytics Dashboard 📊

## Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, and employee retention patterns. The solution consists of **two interactive dashboards** designed to help HR teams understand workforce trends and identify factors contributing to employee turnover.

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. HR Analytics Dashboard
Provides a high-level overview of workforce metrics and attrition analysis.
**KPIs:**
* Total Employees
* Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate (%)
* Average Employee Age
* Average Years at Company
**Visualizations:**
* Attrition by Department
* Attrition by Gender
* Attrition by Marital Status
* Attrition by Business Travel
* Attrition by Job Role
### 2. HR Insights Dashboard
Focuses on deeper workforce insights and retention analysis.
**Visualizations & Insights:**
* Attrition by Job Satisfaction
* Attrition by Work-Life Balance
* Attrition by Overtime
* Attrition by Education
* Employee Tenure Analysis
* Key Business Insights Summary
## Dataset Information
The dataset contains employee information such as:
* Age
* Gender
* Department
* Job Role
* Business Travel
* Marital Status
* Job Satisfaction
* Work-Life Balance
* Performance Rating
* Years at Company
* Attrition Status
## Key Insights
* Employee attrition rate is approximately 16%.
* Research & Development department experiences the highest attrition.
* Frequent business travel is associated with higher employee turnover.
* Sales Executives and Laboratory Technicians show the highest attrition levels.
* Employees working overtime are more likely to leave the organization.
* Average employee age is approximately 37 years.
* Average employee tenure is approximately 7 years.
